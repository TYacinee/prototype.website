Introduction

Video games are becoming increasingly popular around the world, attracting people of all ages and backgrounds. Yet, within this vast industry, one particular category has started to rise to prominence: competitive gaming, or esports. In major tournaments, players face off in different games to determine the best of the best. However, for many newcomers, whether they are already familiar with gaming or complete beginners, the competitive scene can feel overwhelming, leading some to give up early. Among the numerous titles that dominate the esports landscape, Rocket League stands out as one of the most dynamic and accessible. Combining the mechanics of football with the physics of rocket-powered cars, the game has built a massive international following. Its simple concept, yet incredibly high skill ceiling, make it both easy to learn and difficult to master an ideal formula for competitive play. With major tournaments, professional leagues, and a passionate global community, Rocket League has become a flagship example of how a game can thrive in the esports world. Studying and developing tools around it, therefore, provides valuable insights into performance analysis, player development, and the broader dynamics of competitive gaming.

Objective

This project aims to address that challenge by creating a kind of virtual coach. By analyzing gameplay and providing personalized advice, this coach will help players gain a better understanding of competitive gaming. It will offer them guidance on strategies, mechanics, and overall performance, making the esports world more accessible not only to experienced gamers but also to those who are just beginning to explore it.

The EVA Coach

EVA will help both newcomers and experienced players enhance their performance in Rocket League. Powered by thousands of gameplay data points, this AI-driven assistant analyzes individual playing styles to deliver tailored feedback and actionable insights. Players can provide their own replays or specific areas they wish to improve, and EVA will respond with precise recommendations and ranging from strategic positioning to mechanical execution. What makes EVA stand out is its adaptability and accessibility. Unlike traditional coaching, which often requires human availability and high costs, EVA offers continuous, personalized guidance to anyone, anytime.
